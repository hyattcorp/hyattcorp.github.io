Our mission — strategy + execution

HyattCorp was incorporated in 2016 with the vision of providing quality IT consultancy and engineering expertise to our customers in the central London area on a consultancy basis.

We offer a wide range of technologies and services such as: Migrations and upgrades, Exchange\O365, Azure, VMware, Networking, Storage, Security and vulnerability remediation, a wide range of 3rd party applications

Areas of expertise

IT security and support

An ad-hoc resource for you to get over any IT speed bumps you encounter as smoothly as possible. With experience in a wide range of products and implementations it is likely that we will have seen similar if not the same issues meaning we are best placed to assist you.

Disaster recovery

A step-by-step disaster recovery strategies for recovering disrupted systems and networks to minimize negative impacts to company operations though running a risk assessment identifying potential threats to the IT infrastructure. Back-Ups assistance to restore your computer system as it was at a given point in time.

Transfomation

With IT constantly being in a state of flux due to advances in technology and new ways of doing things, transformation is an ongoing task. Whether you are moving premises, moving datacentres or moving to the cloud, we can provide assistance and expertise from an experienced team.

Migrations/upgrades

We are experts replicating applications from one product in to another and leverage the new features when required or replacing your existing software with a newer version of the same product within our upgrade methodology.
